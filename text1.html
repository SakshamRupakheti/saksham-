About Us

We have been in the media industry for over a decade and have established ourselves as a trusted source of information and entertainment.
Kurautey is an innovative media company founded by a group of six students in Nepal. It has established a strong presence on social media platforms such as Facebook and Instagram, as well as on other channels, providing a fresh perspective on news delivery. Kurautey is known for its concise reporting, delivering reliable news from Nepal and around the world in 50 words or less. This approach caters to the modern audience that values quick and accurate information.
